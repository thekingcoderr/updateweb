PRIVACY CONSENT
    DATA SECURITY: 
                    The storage and handling of biometric data pose security 
                risks. If facial recognition databases are compromised, individuals' 
                sensitive information could be exposed, leading to identity theft and 
                other malicious activities.

ACCURACY AND BIAS
    ALGORITHM BIAS: 
     
                Studies have shown that facial recognition systems often have higher 
                error rates for people of color, women, and other marginalized groups. 
                These biases can result in misidentification and discriminatory outcomes.
